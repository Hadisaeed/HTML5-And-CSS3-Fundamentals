Paragraph Tag

Paragraph Tag Example

To create a paragraph, surround the words that make up the paragraph with an opening tag and closing paragraph tag. By default, a browser will show each paragraph on a new line with some space between it and any subsequent paragraphs.

A paragraph consists of one or more sentences that form a self-contained unit of discourse. The start of a paragraph is indicated by a new line
