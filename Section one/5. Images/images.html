Working With Images

A picture can say a thousand words, and great images help make the difference between an average-looking site and a really engaging one Images can be used to set the tone for a site in less time than it takes to read a description. If you do not have photographs to use on your website, there are companies who sell stock images; these are images you pay to use.
Images should...

  • Be relevant
  • Convey information
  • Convey the right mood
  • Be instantly recognisable
  • Fit the color palette
